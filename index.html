Berthine Nyunga Mpinda

I am currently serving as a Tutor at AIMS Cameroon, with particular attention to Data Science. I completed master’s degree in Machine Intelligence from African Institute for Mathematical Sciences, AIMS Ghana. Prior to joining AIMS Ghana, I completed my master's degree in Mathematical Science from the Stellenbosch University, South Africa. I obtained my bachelor degree in Applied Mathematics at the University of Kinshasa, DR Congo. I have supported the establishment of the Women in Machine Learning and Data Science Kinshasa chapter, where Machine Learning skills are being passed on to young girls to simulate their interest in STEM.
